Agents Join the Workflow

我不只是让 AI 写代码。
我让每个阶段留下设计理由。
让每次修改留下影响分析。
让代码、文档、测试共同服务于项目理论。

I don't just let AI write code. Every stage has a design rationale. Every change has an impact analysis. Code, docs, and tests serve the project theory together.
